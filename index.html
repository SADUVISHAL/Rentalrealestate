Our Identity

About Rental Real Estate

Bringing convenience, affordability, and transparency to properties and home services.

Your Trusted Housing Partner

Rental Real Estate is dedicated to helping individuals find quality rental properties and PG accommodations. We remove broker intervention, allowing direct connection to verified owners to save you thousands in middleman fees.

In addition, our company arranges certified home maintenance technicians (electricians, plumbers, deep cleaners) right to your doorstep, making property management simple, affordable, and trustworthy for both tenants and landlords.

Whether you are a student moving to a new city, a professional looking for a furnished workspace flat, or a property owner seeking qualified tenants, Rental Real Estate has custom plans for everyone.

Our Mission

To make rental property search and local home maintenance services simple, affordable, transparent, and completely broker-free across all urban neighborhoods.

Our Vision

To establish a verified housing ecosystem where tenants can search, inspect, book flats or PGs, and coordinate maintenance chores completely inside a unified app dashboard.
